About domestic cats

Cat Senses

    The lions

    Present in Africa and India, the lion typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in dense foressts. It is usually more diurnal than other big cats, but when persecuted adapts to being active at night and at twilight. A lion pride consists of a few adult males, related females and cubs. Prides vary in size and composition from three to 20 adult lions, depeneding on habit and prey availablity. Females cooperate when hunting and prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelope, deer, buffalo, zebra and even giraffe.
